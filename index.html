Travel to Norrköping

Norrköping is a beautiful city by Motala Ström.

Chimneys of old factories can be seen breaking the water surface after a flood in 2009 caused by global warming.

Places to visit

  • Åbackarna
  • Kneippen
  • Industrilandskapet

Things to do

  • Visualiseringscenter C
  • Norrköpings Konstmuseum
  • Arbetets museum
